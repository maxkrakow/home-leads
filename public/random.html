Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HomeLeads Pro

How accurate are your homeowner leads?

Our leads go through a triple verification process, ensuring over 95% accuracy. We regularly update our database and remove any outdated information.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.

What CRM systems do you integrate with?

We integrate with all major CRM systems including Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, and many others. We also offer API access for custom integrations.

How often is your database updated?

Our database is updated daily with new homeowner information. We continuously verify and clean our data to ensure you're working with the most current information.

Do you offer training on how to use the platform?

Yes, all plans include access to our knowledge base and video tutorials. Professional and Enterprise plans also include personalized onboarding sessions.

What information is included with each lead?

Each lead includes homeowner name, property address, contact information (phone/email), property details, estimated value, equity position, and purchase history.
